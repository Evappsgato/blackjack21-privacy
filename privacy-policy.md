# Privacy Policy – BlackJack21

**Last updated:** June 10, 2025

## 1. Information We Collect
BlackJack21 does not collect personal information. The app stores game statistics and premium subscription status locally on your device using a local database (Room).

## 2. Advertising
The free version displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 3. Premium Subscription
Premium status is stored locally on your device. No payment data is processed or stored by this app.

## 4. Internet Access
Free users require an internet connection for ads. Premium users can use the app offline.
